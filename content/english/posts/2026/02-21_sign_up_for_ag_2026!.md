It’s that time again!
Absolut Gotland is back on May 7-10 2026, and it’s time for the Computer Science students to go once again. If you’ve missed out before, take the chance to join this year! AG is a legendary multi-day party for university and college students that has been organized for over 20 years. The event takes place on Gotland, where an entire cottage village and campsite by the coast are booked before the regular season opening.
