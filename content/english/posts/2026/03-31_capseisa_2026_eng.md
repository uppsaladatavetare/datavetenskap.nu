Spring is finally arriving in Uppsala, which also means it’s time for the festive event Capseisa. It is known as the Swedish Championship in pair caps and has been held since 1999.

As tradition dictates, the “Uppsala rules” apply.

As mentioned earlier, Capseisa is played in pairs, so bring a friend and sign up together.

There are a limited number of spots available. Registration opens on April 1st, so you’ll need to be quick to secure a place.

The registration fee is 120 SEK (including 2 patches), and teams have until April 21st to pay before their spots are released to any reserves.

More information can be found on the Facebook event page!
